FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I download the app?

To download the app, visit the App Store or Google Play Store on your iOS or Android device, search for "Gloria Jean's Coffees" app, and download it.

How can I track my orders?

You can view and track your current orders from the "My Orders" section within the app.

How do I create a user account?

After downloading the app, you can sign up by entering the required information to create your user account.

How can I securely store my credit card information in the app?

Your credit card information is securely encrypted and stored through secure payment processors, ensuring your security.

How can I make payments through the app?

You can easily make payments using the payment options available within the app, using your preferred payment method.

What types of coffee can I order through the app?

You can place orders for various coffee beverages and snacks by browsing the menu within the app.

What is the loyalty program and how can I join?

The loyalty program allows you to earn points on purchases made through the app. To join the program, visit the "Loyalty Program" section within the app.

How can I report an issue I encountered in the app?

If you encounter any issues, you can contact us through the "Help" or "Contact" section within the app.
